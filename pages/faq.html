Frequently Asked Questions

Have a question? may our FAQs help you

1. How pixel coders can help me to develop my website

We are a team of web designers and developers. We help you to create an online presence and by presence we don't just mean to design a web site and put it on the net, but design a web site that is a marketable product in its own right.

We create web sites to market your product actively to your customers. It needs study of your process to come up with vibrant ideas.

2. What is your development process and methodology?

At pixel coders, we think 'What to design for whom is more important'.

When we design a custom web site, we have a detailed discussion session with customer about customer's exact requirement and his ideas about his presence on web. What are his targets he wants to achieve by a web site and also about accessory requirements like other support web applications.

3. Do pixel coders provide maintenance for already built websites?

Yes, we offer maintenance for sites we built and also for sites which are already built on per /week or /hr charges depending on complexity and maintenance work involved.

4. Do pixel coders provide web hosting?

Yes, we provide hosting if customer asks for it with additional charges.

Our template packs are available as ready to install at your domain server. We have also tested our template packs at our sub domain before delivery.

5. How much will it cost?

Costing of customer websites depends on complexity and number of pages involved in website.

We analyze customer requirements and reply with our delivery package and its expected cost before start of process.

6. How long does it take to open the web site from placing the firm order?

Our small business website template packs are ready to integrate packs. They can be delivered to client within 48 hrs after customer selects template.

Web sites designed as per customer requirement will take time as per projects complexity and expected time can be estimated after discussing exact requirements with customer.

7. How can I communicate with Pixel coders?

We have contact section with our page (refer: www.pixelcoders.in/contact) to contact us or you can email us at: pixelcoders@hotmail.com, pixelcoders@gmail.com or can refer to our social networks to stay connected.

8. Do you offer any type of discounts?

Yes, we offer discounts for educational and non-profit organizations.

We are the partner for brands who want to improve their user's experience
